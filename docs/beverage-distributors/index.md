# Beverage Distributors (Soda, Water, Juices, etc.)

Many distributors in Kenya lose their businesses not from low sales, but from **small, untracked losses** that build up over time: missing crates, unreturned empties, unreconciled cash, staff debts, overstocking slow-movers, and poor record-keeping.  

**DistriHub changes that.**  
It gives you full visibility and control over products, returnables, staff conduct, money, and compliance — all in one system. Offline-first, mobile-supported, eTIMS-integrated, and built for real Kenyan challenges.

## Core Problems We Solve for Beverage Distributors

- **Lack of proper record-keeping** → Everything is tracked automatically: stock movements, sales, returns, debts.
- **Returnable empties (crates, bottles) losses** → Strict tracking at every step (outbound full → inbound empty). If less empties return than expected → auto deposit check or staff debt created (must clear before payroll).
- **Staff accountability & remuneration** → Shifts required before POS use or loading. Permanent, casual (daily processing), commission-based payroll in one click — auto-deducts debts/shortages.
- **Reconciliation failures** → Load reconciliation compares POS sales + unsold returns + empties. Money reconciliation checks sales vs cash. No close until balanced — flags issues instantly.
- **Stock & inventory leaks** → No direct stock edits. All movements via sales, returns, adjustments, transfers (with approvals). Mandatory stock-taking every 2 days — variances resolved quickly.
- **Overstocking / slow-movers** → AI learns trends → advises on fast/slow products to avoid dead stock.
- **Cash & debt mismatches** → Auto staff debts for shortages/missing empties. Deducted from payroll/commission.
- **Compliance & reporting** → Real-time eTIMS submission, full books (COGS, revenue, expenses), P&L, balance sheet, net investment reports. Statutory scoring (NSSF, SHA, etc.).
- **Other leaks** → Fleet management (maintenance/insurance reminders via SMS), supplier payments, procurement (PO → GRN), expense management, customer discounts/loyalty.

## How DistriHub Works for Your Beverage Operation

1. **Shift & Load Control**  
   - In-store shift (walk-ins) or route shift (field sales) must start before POS or loading.  
   - All sales attached to shift + staff → full accountability.

2. **Loading & Field Sales**  
   - Create load sheet → load full crates → track van stock.  
   - Sales via mobile POS (offline ok) → auto deposits added.

3. **Returns & Empties**  
   - Record empties returned → credit deposits.  
   - System audits: expected vs actual. Shortage → deposit required or auto staff debt.

4. **Reconciliation (Load + Money)**  
   - Load: POS sales + unsold returns + empties must align → can't close otherwise.  
   - Money: sales vs cash collected. Shortage → staff debt (deducted from pay).

5. **Payroll & Staff**  
   - One-click run: basic + allowances + commissions - debts.  
   - Casuals processed daily.

6. **Stock & AI Insights**  
   - Strict policies (FIFO, expiry avoidance, no direct edits).  
   - AI advises fast/slow movers, route planning (traffic/weather — improving), monthly budgets.

7. **Extra Tools**  
   - Customer portal for orders → better route planning.  
   - Pack breaking/bundling (crate → singles, gift packs).  
   - Permission-based access, dashboards per staff, fleet/insurance alerts, supplier management.

## Pro Tips to Avoid Failure

!!! danger "Common Failure Points Fixed"
    - Missing crates/empties → tracked & audited end-to-end.
    - Unpaid staff debts → auto-created & deducted.
    - Overstock → AI prevents it.
    - Compliance fines → real-time eTIMS + scoring.
    - No records → full books & reports always ready.

!!! tip "Start Small, Scale Fast"
    - Begin with shift control & reconciliation — biggest quick wins.
    - Use customer portal early for order visibility.
    - Let AI learn (after a few months) for smarter stocking/routing.
